## 1. Automated Lead Qualification

Perfect for:

- Real estate  
- EdTech  
- Coaching  
- Service businesses  

AI agents can:

- read incoming WhatsApp/website leads  
- extract user intent  
- score the lead  
- tag it as hot/warm/cold  
- assign to the correct team  

This alone saves **5–10 hours/week**.
